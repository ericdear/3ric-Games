Strict Browser Privacy Policy



We don’t collect any personal data


We don’t collect any search queries

When you search, your query is searched using google’s search engine. The data is not collected by us.


What happens to your data beyond Strict Browser once you make a search

Once you make a search, you leave our privacy protection. This is true for any search result or any urls you visit.


We don’t disclose or sell your contact info

When you contact us through email, we will only use the contact information to contact you back. Your contact information will never be disclosed or sold.
